About the Women Safety Band

The Women Safety Band is designed to help women feel safe wherever they go. With a single press of a button, the band can send SOS alerts, share live location, and notify trusted contacts instantly.

This application connects the band with your smartphone to enable advanced safety features like real-time location tracking, dark mode, and direct communication with family members.
