Quick Safety Tips

  • 🚶‍♀️ Stay in well-lit and populated areas at night.
  • 📱 Keep your phone fully charged and carry a power bank.
  • 👩‍👩‍👧 Share your location with trusted contacts when traveling.
  • 🚕 Use verified ridesharing or public transport services.
  • ⚠️ Trust your instincts and avoid unsafe situations.
